# iOS Development from a .NET Background

As a seasoned .NET developer, transitioning to iOS development presented both challenges and opportunities. In this post, I'll share my journey and the strategies I used to accelerate my learning process.

## The Swift Learning Curve

Coming from C#, Swift felt both familiar and foreign. Here are some key differences and similarities I encountered:

- **Optionals**: Swift's approach to null safety is more explicit than C#'s nullable types
- **Protocol-Oriented Programming**: While similar to interfaces, protocols in Swift are more powerful
- **Value Types**: Swift's structs are value types by default, unlike C# classes

## Leveraging AI for Learning
